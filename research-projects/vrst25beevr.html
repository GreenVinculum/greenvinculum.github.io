Teleoperated 360◦ Video Capture of Beehives for Scientific Visualization in VR

Charles Ahn 0009-0005-7493-3641

George Mason University

Abstract

Studying bee behavior and diagnosing hive issues in their natural habitats has often been a challenge due to limited visibility, observer interference, and limitations of conventional recording methods. A teleoperated immersive 360$^{\circ}$ view offers an unobstrusive, holistic observation within the hive. In this work, we present a framework for capturing and analyzing bee activity using commercially available 360$^{\circ}$ cameras and hardware to create an immersive VR experience.
